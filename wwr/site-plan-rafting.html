Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help potential clients to get information about the water rafting services we provide and the various times they can book a reservation for participating. This site makes available the contact of the organisation for the potential clients to make further enquiries. The company needs this website to increase it chances of being known and subsequently patronised by future rafters and as aresult increases the company's revenue.

Audience

Our principal audiences are teenagers and adults with their families seeking to spend a vacation together. We ancipate that organisations for youth development and education to organize events for their members during holidays and we are there to provide them with best environment for their recreational activities. our audience will usually access our website using mobile devices and laptops. Since most of our audience are youth and young adults, our content, colors, etc will be vibrant and inviting to these young and middle-aged groups.
