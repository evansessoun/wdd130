Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show potential employers and freelancing clients my skillsets and other projects I have worked on. The site will provide my contact information to potential clients and employers. It will also link my other social pages such as linkedIn and facebook page to provide additional information to employers, clients and other developers when needed.

Audience

The audience are future employers, freelancing clients and other web developers who may want to collaborate with me to work on projects. The audience will mostly access the site over a laptop computer and occassionaly over mobile devices. Because most of the audience will be young adult and middle-aged group in professional setups, the content and colors will be moderately vibrant and more professional.
